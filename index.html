About the seminar

This seminar takes place on Thursdays from 2:30 PM to 3:30 PM at the USTC Shanghai Institute for Advanced Studies.

The presentations last one hour.

This seminar focuses on the following topics:

  • Complex geometry
  • Algebraic geometry
  • Riemannian geometry and analysis
  • Complex dynamics

You can write to the organizers if you are interested in the seminar; we are looking for speakers.

After the seminar, a discussion session with tea is organized at the institute.

Everyone is welcome!
